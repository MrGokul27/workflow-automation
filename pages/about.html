Our Evolution

From a garage-born kernel of an idea to a global automation standard.

2018: The Kernel

Stackly was founded by a team of three ex-AWS engineers frustrated with the fragility of custom scripts. We built the first prototype of our visual logic engine in a small shared office in Berlin.

2020: Series A & Scale

Despite a global shift to remote work, our tool became essential for distributed teams. We closed $25M in funding to expand our cloud-native automation capabilities.

2023: Enterprise Standard

Stackly is now deployed across 30% of Fortune 500 tech stacks, managing billions of production events every day with military-grade precision.

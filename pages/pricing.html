Billing & Logistics FAQ

Can I switch between plans at any time? expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, the change is immediate and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my workflow limit? expand_more

On the Starter plan, you'll receive a notification 24 hours before your workflows are paused. On Professional and Enterprise plans, we offer unlimited workflows so you never have to worry about hitting a hard cap.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or open source? expand_more

Absolutely. We support the community. Verified non-profits and registered open-source projects are eligible for a 50% discount on any Professional plan. Contact our support team to apply.

How secure is my billing information? expand_more

We use Stripe for all payment processing. Your credit card information never touches our servers. We are fully PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant.
